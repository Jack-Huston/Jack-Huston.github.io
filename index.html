About Me

I'm an aerospace engineer specializing in rocket propulsion systems with a focus on large liquid rocket engines. My expertise spans combustion chamber design, regenerative cooling systems, turbopump development, and high-pressure propellant feed systems for LOX/RP-1 and LOX/LH2 engines.

Currently pursuing opportunities in heavy-lift launch vehicle propulsion, I'm passionate about pushing the boundaries of rocket engine performance through innovative thermal management, advanced materials, and optimized combustion efficiency. My goal is to contribute to the next generation of engines powering humanity's journey to orbit and beyond.

Outside the test stand, I'm an avid mountaineer, rock climber, and skier.
