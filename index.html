About Me

I'm an aerospace engineer with experience in spacecraft propulsion analysis, system modeling, and test data automation. At Lockheed Martin, I work on hypergolic propulsion systems across their full fleet of spacecraft supporting thruster testing, contamination analysis, valve & filter component procurement, and subsystem modeling for Earth-orbit, lunar, and deep-space missions. I enjoy solving complex engineering problems and getting hands-on with the details that bring real hardware to life and keep it performing reliably.

I aim to develop reusable liquid propulsion systems that make spaceflight more frequent, reliable, and efficient. My goal is to apply my background in modeling, testing, and system analysis to the design, build, and validation of next-generation launch vehicle engines that enable rapid and repeatable access to orbit.
